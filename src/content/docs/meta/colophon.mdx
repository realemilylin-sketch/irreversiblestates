---

This site defines terms, structures, and reference materials related to **irreversible states** in real-world processes.

It does not adjudicate disputes, provide legal advice, or determine outcomes.

---

## Scope

The materials published here are intended to:

- define controlled terminology,
- describe process-level structures,
- illustrate reference cases for discussion and analysis.

They are **informative**, not prescriptive.

---

## Authority and Limitations

This site does not:

- assign fault or liability,
- determine legal or evidentiary admissibility,
- replace contractual, regulatory, or judicial processes.

Any reference to finality describes a **process-level condition**, not a legal determination.

---

## Stability

Definitions and specifications are intended to remain stable once published.  
Revisions, when necessary, are versioned and documented.
